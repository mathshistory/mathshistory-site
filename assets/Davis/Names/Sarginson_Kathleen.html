Sarginson, Kathleen

Kathleen Sarginson
(Married name: Collard P J) graduated from the University of London (Holloway College) in 1937
with a BSc INT (Class: I)

Further details:
M.Sc.1939, Ph.D.(Edin) 1941. STAFF S'hampton 1941-42, Somerville 1942-55, Professor Ibadan 1956-65, OBE, Nottingham 1965-81, Papua New Guinea Chair 1981-86

Attended the EMS St Andrews Colloquium in 1938.
