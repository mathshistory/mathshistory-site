Wrinch, Dorothy

Dorothy Maud Wrinch
(Married name: Glaser O C) graduated from the University of Cambridge (Girton College) in 1916
with a BA (Class: I)

Further details:
M.Sc.,D.Sc.(Lond) 1919, 1921. D.Sc.(Oxon) 1929. Res. Fellow Girton 1922-24 STAFF UCL 1918-20, LMH, St. Hilda's 1923-39, Science Depts. in USA 1939-
